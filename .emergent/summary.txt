<analysis>**original_problem_statement:**
The user wants to build a comprehensive marketplace application for Angola, initially named Kandengue and later renamed to TudoAqui, to be developed by Sincesoft-Sinceridade Service.

PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:
1.  **Modules:**
    *   **Taxi:** Price comparison with existing apps (e.g., Tupuca, Mano), with an interactive GPS map feature similar to Waze. Users should be able to choose between TudoAqui's navigation or another app.
    *   **Restaurant:** Integration with existing delivery systems like Tupuca and Mano.
    *   **Tourism:** A directory for tourist spots (parks, museums, resorts, hotels) with information, pricing, and a reservation system.
    *   **Real Estate (Mixeiro):** A B2B and B2C platform for renting and selling properties and land.
2.  **Payments:** Integration with Angolan payment systems, including Multicaixa Express, Unitelmoney, and BAI Paga.
3.  **User & Partner System:**
    *   A complete B2B/B2C system.
    *   Two user tiers (Normal, Premium) and three partner tiers with modular service access.
    *   Partners should have control over their published services and be able to interact with customers.
    *   Partner wallet for managing funds (credit/debit).
4.  **Accounting & Fiscal Compliance:**
    *   An integrated accounting module for partners based on the Angolan General Chart of Accounts (PGCA), featuring Journal, Ledger, Trial Balance, and Balance Sheet.
    *   The application must adhere to Angolan fiscal regulations.
5.  **Authentication:** Secure login system, including Google Auth and authentication using valid documents for partners.

**User's preferred language**: Portuguese
